About Us

Cardiotronics is an Australian biomedical engineering company providing both clinical and home-based equipment and services certified to ISO9001:2015. Our vision is to develop innovating cardiology medical equipment with uppermost quality unmatched by others whilst changing the lives of patients who are at risk or are living with a cardiovascular disease, particularly in rural communities.

Engineering new possibilities

We’re dedicated to researching and developing novel diagnostic and monitoring equipment, from software development to personal and commercial use biomedical sensors.

Medical Equipment Services

We have extensive experience in providing high-standard servicing needs including equipment calibration, software updates, equipment repairs and annual equipment maintenance.

PC and App Intergration

From delivering patient-centric care via telehealth, automated outcome data collection to engaging in 4,000+ peer-reviewed exercises, PulseLogistics® will never let you lose track in your health journey.

Home-based Equipment

All our products comply with the Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA) to ensure that individuals receive outstanding accuracy and safety. Our wearables, portable sensors and diagnostic devices can instantly deliver consistent and precise medical information to your local medical professional.
